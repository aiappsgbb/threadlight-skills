SPEC.md FSI · claims-triage · v0.3

Claims triage — route by complexity.

Inbound retail-banking claims arrive via two channels. The agent ranks them on complexity and regulator exposure, then routes each one. Anything above the bar lands in a human queue with reasoning attached.

Process

  • Ingest claim text + structured metadata from the case system.
  • Score complexity (1–5) and regulator exposure (flag).
  • Route: auto-respond, assist-agent, or escalate.
  • Persist the routing decision & reasoning to the audit ledger.

HITL gates

  • Every escalate requires a human signature before the customer reply.
  • Every regulator-exposed claim is mirrored to a 100% review queue.
