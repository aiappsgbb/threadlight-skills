threadlight-production-ready
Advisory · soft-gate Hand-off package

Take a pilot that just passed threadlight-safe-check --phase post-deploy and walk it across 13 cross-cutting production-readiness pillars — network, agent governance, identity, secrets, observability, continuous evals, responsible AI, HITL, supply chain, cost, reliability, SRE handover, model lifecycle. Posture defaults to AI Citadel spoke; falls back to Foundry-native (core only) or a customer-owned gateway when SPEC § 12 declares a different target.
